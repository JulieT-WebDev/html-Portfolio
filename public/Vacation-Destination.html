Three Destinations Worth Visiting

According to Julissa Trabal!

  1. Alaska

    I loved Alaska for its stunning views and majestic glaciers - it truly feels like a different world.

  2. Puerto Rico

    Puerto Rico offers beautiful mountains, spectacular beaches full of natural beauty and tranquility, and of course, unforgettable food.

  3. Aruba

    Aruba boasts soft white sand, turquoise waters, and some of the friendliest locals you'll ever meet.
